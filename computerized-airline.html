Airline computer systems do the following

Maintaining and operating the airline’s reservation system and passenger name records. The system holds your passenger names and personal information and its where your flight reservations are kept.

They communicate with global distribution systems which offers agents with a list of all available flights from all airlines. Still, airline computer system allows the agents to connect with them and book flights directly.

NOTE : If this system crushes, then airlines can’t operate.

Benefits of a computerized airline

It records the passenger’s information for reference.

The computer system makes it easy to book car hire online and get a great price through bargain.

It also offers online hotel bookings.

Provides the flight schedule information i.e. days and times of departure for flights.

Control passenger’s aircrafts and vehicles.
