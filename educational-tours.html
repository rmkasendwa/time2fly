Educational Tours

These are usually planned combination of tours, visit sites and hands-on learning opportunities with the targets of global learning which can enhance knowledge of students, holiday makers, and other tourists.

Some of the benefits with educational tours include

They help tourists to learn through visual experience and can be an interesting way to explore new things,

Carrier choices can be enhanced through educational tours,

Students impart new knowledge and skills by using new concepts they learnt from these educational tours.

Some of the Educational tour Centres found in Uganda are

Ndere center located along Ntinda - kisaasi road,

Uganda wild life conservation education center in Entebbe,

Uganda museum,

Association Of Uganda Tour Operators
