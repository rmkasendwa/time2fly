We do most of our reservations through Galileo reservation and ticketing system which has a worldwide recognition among the airlines.

Galileo GDS (Global Distribution System) is a computer reservations system (CRS) owned by Travel port. As of 2000 it had a 26.4% share of worldwide CRS airline bookings. In addition to airline reservations, the Galileo CRS is also used to book train travel, cruises, car rental, and hotel rooms. Other common GDS systems include; Amadeus, Sabre, and Worldspan.

The company regularly purchases updates for the program which helps to minimize the time spent on the request processing and leads to more convenient and prompt passenger service.
